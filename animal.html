Eastern cottontail

Rabbits are cool.

The adult Eastern cottontail weighs 2.5 pounds as an adult.

They are usually brown with a bit of white on the fur.

The population for Eastern cottontail is growing and it is a common rabbit in many places.

They have four legs

The size of the rabbits varies from 15.5 inches to 18.75 inches

They live up to 3 years in the wild
